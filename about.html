Our Story

Founded with a mission to deliver quality medicines, MEKSTONE PHARMACEUTICALS combines scientific rigor with manufacturing excellence. Our focus is on accessible health solutions across multiple therapeutic segments including general medicine, pediatrics, and nutraceuticals.

We partner with leading WHO–GMP certified manufacturers and perform strict QA at every step — from raw material testing to finished product release. Our products are manufactured by industry-leading partners including PACE BIOTECH for injectables, STABICOAT VITAMINS for nutrition products, and SANCTUS GLOBAL FORMULATIONS LTD for PPI's and anti-diabetic medications, and IVM PHARMACIA additional pharmaceutical formulations.

With years of experience and a dedicated team of professionals, we continue to expand our product portfolio while maintaining the highest standards of quality and safety through our trusted manufacturing partnerships.

Mission & Vision

Vision: Vision is not seeing things as they are but by seeing them as they will be. Our Vision is to be one of the best pharmaceutical companies by providing high quality, affordable and innovative solutions in medicine.

Mission: Our mission is to discover, develop and successfully market the pharmaceutical products to prevent, diagnose, alleviate and cure diseases. Our entire efforts are towards providing total customer satisfaction through excellence in technology and services.

Our ultimate aim is 'Whatever we do, it should be beneficial to all the people, society and nation'. Our values guide every decision we make, ensuring that patient safety and product quality remain our top priorities in all our operations.

Our Manufacturing Partners

At MEKSTONE PHARMACEUTICALS LLP, we are proud to partner with leading manufacturers who share our commitment to quality and excellence. Our products are manufactured by these industry-leading companies, ensuring the highest standards of safety, efficacy, and compliance.

Injectables
PACE BIOTECH
Our injectable products are manufactured by PACE BIOTECH, a leading manufacturer specializing in sterile injectable formulations. They maintain the highest standards of aseptic processing and quality control, ensuring safe and effective injectable medications.
Nutrition
STABICOAT VITAMINS
STABICOAT VITAMINS is our trusted partner for nutrition products. They specialize in advanced vitamin formulations and nutritional supplements, utilizing cutting-edge technology to ensure optimal bioavailability and stability.
PPI's & Anti-Diabetic
SANCTUS GLOBAL FORMULATIONS LTD
SANCTUS GLOBAL FORMULATIONS LTD manufactures our PPI's (Proton Pump Inhibitors) and Anti-Diabetic medications. They are renowned for their expertise in complex formulations and adherence to international quality standards.
IV Fluids
IVM PHARMACIA
IVM Pharmacia is a trusted pharmaceutical manufacturer known for its commitment to quality, safety, and innovation. With state-of-the-art facilities and strict compliance with GMP standards, they specialize in producing a wide range of high-quality formulations.

Quality Control
